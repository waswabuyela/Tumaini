SPORTING DIRECTOR

EDU

Nationality: Brazilian.

The Brazilian was part of the Invincibles side and played five seasons for the Gunners. Returned to the club to take up this new role in July 2019, following three years as general co-ordinator for the Brazil national team.

MANAGER :

MIKEL ARTETA

Nationality: Spanish.

Spent five seasons as a player at Arsenal after stints with Rangers and Everton. Brought to Manchester City by Pep Guardiola in the summer of 2016. Was appointed Arsenal manager on December 20th 2019, having previously been interviewed for the job when Unai Emery was chosen.

ASSISTANT :

ALBERT STUIVENBERG

Nationality: Dutch.
Was number two to Louis Van Gaal at Manchester United and also managed Genk in Belgium. Will continue to work for Wales alongside manager Ryan Giggs until the end of Euro 2020.

CARLOS CUESTA

Nationality: Spanish
Only 25, but already a Uefa Pro Licence holder who has worked for Atletico Madrid and Juventus. Got to know Arteta during a study visit with Manchester City in August 2018.

MIGUEL MOLINA

Nationality: Spanish.

Hired from Atletico Madrid. An expert at integrating the physical and mental elements of the game into training sessions.
